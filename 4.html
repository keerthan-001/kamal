Gourmet Seafood Paella

Overview

Our Gourmet Seafood Paella is a traditional Spanish dish that brings the flavors of the Mediterranean to your plate. It is a vibrant and aromatic rice dish that is perfect for sharing.

Ingredients

  • Arborio rice
  • Fresh shrimp
  • Calamari
  • Mussels
  • Green peas
  • Bell peppers
  • Saffron
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Chicken broth

Preparation Method

We start by saut√©ing garlic and bell peppers in olive oil, followed by adding the Arborio rice to toast it slightly. Then, we add chicken broth, saffron, and let it simmer. Finally, we incorporate the seafood and cook until tender.

Serving Suggestions

Serve hot, garnished with fresh parsley and lemon wedges. Pair it with a chilled white wine for an exquisite dining experience.
